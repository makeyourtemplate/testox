And they are building a strange robot. They said they will give It to Kouji Kabuto.

I will never give in, even if It means dying.

But I must give It up, or our world will end.

Well, even if we give you a high dose, It will just make you sluggish for a full day.

give It two or three years, and you will only have some superficial knowledge.
