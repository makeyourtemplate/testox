Using 'Because what' in a sentence

'Because what' in a Sentence

  • I'll be all right, Because what you just said makes me the happiest girl in the world.
  • Because what it was connected to has been ignored.

'Because what' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • Because what protects you then from the slings and arrows?
  • It's just not a solution that I'm willing to teach my daughter Because what happens next time?

'Because what' in a Suggested Sentence

  • I've come here Because I was concerned with what he had told me.
  • Because of what Nike said, I will do my best to protect you.
