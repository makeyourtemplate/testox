Using 'I would' in a sentence

'I would' in a Sentence

  • I would want to see something else too.
  • I would steal everything from him.

'I would' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • I said I would be back to take it, right?
  • Do you think I would do something like that?

'I would' in a Suggested Sentence

  • The letter that I sent you, said that you would be present on the day of my wedding.
  • I think Keith would stand out if he set foot outside.
